If you're looking for info about testing the Hyde Park Gym Workout App, please go here.


Almost done with the Hyde Park Workout App. It's been a long road to hoe, but I'm rather pleased with the results. I've been working on it so long, styles in the Android community have changed (Google has been catching up with me!). Also, I need to make some major changes for fragments and tablets.

Another app I'm doing that is very cool is called Austin Underground. It's a tour of all those amazing yet unknown places in Austin that I've discovered while living here my whole life. Think of it as a scavenger hunt with stories. It's the sort of thing that'll be better if very few people know about it! Counter-intuitive, no?
