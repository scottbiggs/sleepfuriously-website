Episode 1

Way back in college, an old friend named Ed wrote me a letter.  Along with the hand-written note was a typed story in serial form.  To get the next episode, I had to write back.


I thought it was hilarious.  So did my roommate, the guys across the hall, etc.  WTCJ was copied and passed around.  Everyone was desperate for each new episode; it wasn’t long before strangers started asking me if I’d checked my mail today.
