biggs’ Proximity Info

 
 

Okay, I did it again.  I’m publishing another app.  This is actually something that I needed and used and saved some money with.  It’s a little program that checks out your phone’s hardware and determines if it has a proximity detector or not.  And if it does, it tells you about it.


What’s a proximity detector?  Pretty simple, really.  It’s special hardware that determines if you are talking on your phone or not.  It usually works by shining a little infrared light.  If it bounces off your ear into a light detector, then it assumes you’re talking on your phone.  Otherwise it assumes you’re looking at it. 


This is how your phone knows to turn off the screen while you’re talking and turn it back on again so you can hang up.


I had a phone that was a real bitch to hang up.  Why?  It seemed as if it didn’t know if I was talking or not.  ...some code later, and I noticed that it did not have one of these detectors.  Unacceptable.  Phone returned, and now I have a much better phone that I can actually use.
