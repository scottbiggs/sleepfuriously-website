Biggs’ Timecode Calculator

 
 

Okay, after what I thought was going to be a no-brainer quickie app, I’m finally ready to announce a calculator for people who deal with timecodes (primarily editors, directors, sound designers, etc.).  Whew, not only was the basic algorithm a major hurdle, but because I thought this was going to be easy I coded fast and loose.  Now there’s the payback of bunches of bugs.


So, I’m releasing a Beta version (v. 0.9).  I expect bugs here; so should you.  And tell me about ‘em.  They *will* be fixed.  And then there’ll be the real release, a nice clean version (1.0, yay!).


And yeah, like my other little apps, this will be a no-cost add-free app for the Android.  So ask for it on you Android Market.


App Features:

  1. *Add, Subtract, Multiply, and Divide SMPTE timecodes.

  2. *Set any frame rate from 3 to 99 frames per second.

  3. *Correctly and intuitively works with Drop Frame timecodes, used extensively in NTSC environments.

  4. *Works nicely with built-in keyboards.

  5. *Optionally implements sound and haptic (vibrations) feedback.

  6. *Saves your preferences.
