Apps

 

For several months, I have been teaching myself the fascinating world of Google’s Android operating system.  I have written dozens of little apps--mostly tutorials on the various aspects of this tiny portable computer.


My philosophy is to write little apps that do one thing well.  Make it tight, make it cute, and make it work.  Then let anyone use it.


Bigger programs (like the workout app), well that’s another story.
